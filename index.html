GĚ YŌU TĀN

葛優癱_ is a meme token inspired by Chinese movie star Ge You, known for his iconic slouching pose. The pose, symbolizing reluctance, resignation, and feeling overwhelmed, originated from a screenshot of one of his TV shows. It has since become a popular meme to express a range of emotions on social media. This token celebrates Ge You and resonates with anyone who's ever felt like a "couch potato."

Ge You and His Art of Slouching

Ge You, a retired actor, has mastered the art of doing nothing. He spends his days lounging on his worn-out couch, slouching in a way that would put a teenager to shame. His friends and family have given up trying to get him to do anything productive.

The Slouching Chronicles: Lessons from Ge You

1
The Slouching Interview

One day, a young journalist, eager to interview Ge You about his legendary career, shows up at his doorstep. But instead of getting a insightful conversation, she finds Ge You slouching in his living room, surrounded by empty snack packets and unwashed laundry.

2
Tales from a Slouch

As she tries to rouse him from his slumber, Ge You begins to recount stories of his past, of love, loss, and laughter. The journalist, entranced by his tales, forgets about her deadline and joins him on the couch.

3
Together we slouch

Together, they spend the day slouching, snacking, and reminiscing about life's simple joys. Ge You teaches her that sometimes, doing nothing is the greatest accomplishment of all.
